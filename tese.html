My favorite foods

  1. PIZZA

    2. Pizza is one of the most famous and beloved foods worldwide. It features a crispy and delicious crust topped with tomato sauce and cheese, and you can customize it with ingredients like mushrooms, olives, peppers, meat, and other vegetables. Pizza comes in various shapes and sizes, allowing you to choose according to your preference.

  2. shawerma

    3. Shawarma is a popular and flavorful dish in the Middle East and around the world. It typically consists of slices of grilled meat (such as chicken or lamb) seasoned with delicious spices and wrapped in Arabic bread with vegetables and sauces. The balance of flavors in shawarma between the grilled meat and refreshing sauces makes it an amazing meal.

  3. burger

    4. The burger, or hamburger, is one of the most popular meals in the United States and globally. It features a patty of grilled beef or chicken, often seasoned with various spices, and is placed inside a bun with additional ingredients like cheese, onions, and sauces. Burgers can be customized to suit your personal taste, making them a fun and flavorful meal.
